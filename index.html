About Lak Art

Crafting Nature's Beauty

Welcome to Lak Art, where Shashika Lakshan transforms wood into stunning pieces of art that capture the essence of nature. Each creation is meticulously crafted through traditional wood burning techniques and modern laser engraving.

Our artworks feature environmental themes including wildlife, forests, butterflies, tigers, birds and other natural beauties. Every piece tells a story and brings a touch of nature into your home.
